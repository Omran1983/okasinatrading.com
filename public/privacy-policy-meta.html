Privacy Policy – OKASINA Trading

Last updated: 21 November 2025

OKASINA Trading ("we", "us", "our") operates the OKASINA website and related online services, including integrations with Meta products such as Facebook and Instagram (the "Services"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and protect your information when you interact with our Services.

Information we collect

  • Contact details you provide (such as name, email, phone number, delivery address).
  • Account and order information (products viewed, items purchased, payment status – but not full card numbers).
  • Information from Meta products when you interact with our ads or pages, as allowed by their terms (for example: ad performance, clicks, conversions, anonymised audience statistics).

How we use your information

  • To process and deliver your orders.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your messages.
  • To show you relevant products and offers on our website, Facebook and Instagram.
  • To analyse and improve our marketing campaigns and website performance.

Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with trusted service providers who help us operate our business (for example: payment processors, delivery companies, email/SMS providers, analytics tools) – always under appropriate data protection safeguards.

When we use Meta's advertising tools (such as Facebook/Instagram Ads, pixels or Conversions API), your information may be processed by Meta in accordance with their own terms and privacy policy.

Data retention

We keep your information for as long as necessary to provide our Services and comply with legal obligations. You may contact us to request correction or deletion of your data where applicable.

Your rights

You may contact us at any time to:

  • Access or update your personal information.
  • Request deletion of your customer account where possible.
  • Opt out of marketing messages (email, SMS, WhatsApp) by using the unsubscribe options provided or by contacting us directly.
